GET TO KNOW ME

Howdy! I'm Rachel, a freelance Chicago based designer with an emphasis on visual design. I will be graduating from Columbia College Chicago in Spring 2022 with a bachelor's degree in Graphic Design. I am currently working towards my certificate in UX/UI from UT Austin Bootcamps.

From my experiences in the Bootcamp, as well as my own side passion projects, I enjoy working on design challenges ranging from creating and designing apps, concept creation, user research, and collaborating with other designers in a design sprint.

When I am not studying or designing, I like to rack up points on my Southwest Rapid Rewards and train for my gelato taste testing certificate...don't believe me?
